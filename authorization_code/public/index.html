VIBE METER


ACOUSTICNESS
-------------------------------
The higher the Acousticness bar is the more natural sounds are present.
EX/ Harp Vs. Synth

DANCEABILITY
-------------------------------
The higher the Danceability bar the more sutible it is for dancing.
EX/ Podcasts Vs. EDM

ENERGY
-------------------------------
The higher the Energy the more instense/active it is.
EX/ Fast and Noisy Vs. Soft and Gentle

LOUDNESS
-------------------------------
Loudness corresponds to the average decibels of a song.
EX/ Metal Vs. Lofi

TEMPO
-------------------------------
The higher the Tempo value the more beats per minute
EX/ Fast Rap Vs. Sad Songs

VALENCE
-------------------------------
The higher the Valence the happier/more positive the song is
EX/ Breakup Music Vs. Summer Party


THE VIBE FILTER
-------------------------------
The closer to the "STRICT" side the closer the songs in the playlist must fit the overall "Vibe" of the playlist.
If the filter is near "LAX" then only songs dissimilar the the overall playlist genre are removed.
